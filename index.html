Welcome

Alpha and Omega Prophetic Church is a community of believers who are passionate about the things of God. We are a church that is committed to the teachings of Jesus Christ and the principles of the Bible. we strive hard to reach out to the lost and to make disciples of all nations. We believe that the church is the body of Christ and that we are called to be the hands and feet of Jesus in the world. We are dedicated to worship, prayer, and the study of God's Word and also committed to loving God and loving others. We invite you to join us as we seek to grow in our relationship with God and with one another.
