Our Services:

Church services

we hold our church service every saturday from 10:00am to 2:00pm. we love drawing many souls to Christ and we believe that the church is the body of Christ and that we are all members of one body. we believe in truth and sincerity in our worship. we do holy communion every saturday of the month. we believe that the body and blood of Christ is a symbol of the new covenant that God has made with us.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reach out to the lost and the broken, to bring healing and transformation to the lives of people. To bring hope and love where there is despair and hatred. To bring the light of Christ to a dark world.
